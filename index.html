3. Description of the Bot

The Bot is a multi-purpose Discord application that provides the following features:

3.1 Economy and Progression

  • A coin and gem currency system with wallets, daily rewards, and streaks.
  • A levelling system based on server activity, with rank cards and tiered ranks.
  • Achievements and quest systems with milestone rewards.
  • A bank system with savings accounts, deposits, withdrawals, tiered interest, peer-to-peer transfers, and loans.
  • An economy graph and spending analytics.

3.2 Mini-Games

  • Gambling games: coinflip, slots, roulette, blackjack, hilo, five-dice.
  • Skill games: Wordle, Hangman, Trivia, Riddles, Country Quiz, Emoji Phrase, Unscramble, Mastermind, Memory/Pairs, Math Sprint, Solo Heist, Hide & Seek, Number Hunt, Lottery.
  • A rock-paper-scissors game and a dice roller.

3.3 AI Systems

  • An AI chat system supporting multiple providers (Groq, Cerebras, OpenRouter) and ten personality modes. The AI responds to messages in configured channels.
  • An Akinator-style guessing game powered by AI and a Wikipedia-crawled knowledge base.
  • AI-generated trivia questions, riddles, and country quizzes.

3.4 Voice Features

  • Speech-to-text (STT) transcription via Groq's Whisper API while in voice channels.
  • Text-to-speech (TTS) playback using the Murf API.
  • Opt-in voice cloning, which records a sample of your voice and uses it for TTS output.
  • A soundboard system for playing preset audio clips in voice channels.

3.5 Server Management and Social

  • Customisable welcome and leave messages with canvas-rendered welcome cards, DM messaging, invite tracking, and auto-role assignment.
  • Reaction roles with configurable mappings.
  • Leaderboards for coins, XP, bank balance, achievements, and game stats.
  • A server pet with stats, moods, and interactive events.
  • A time capsule system for scheduling messages to be delivered in the future.
  • Auto-quote channels.
  • GIF reactions (fun and anime categories).
  • Image and media processing tools (filters, captions, overlays, transformations).
  • A guidebook and per-server setup/configuration panel.
  • Custom embed builder and custom command manager.
  • Event scheduling and announcement system.

3.6 Customisation

  • Per-user card personalisation: rank card theme, style, accent colour, and background tint.
  • Per-guild prefix, economy parameters, and embed colour branding.

This Privacy Policy describes how our Discord bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, stores, uses, and protects information obtained from users ("you") who interact with it through Discord servers ("guilds") or through direct messages.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the Bot's features. The categories of data we collect are:

2.1 User-Provided Data

  • Discord User ID — A numeric identifier assigned by Discord. Used to associate your progress, preferences, economy balance, and settings across all features.
  • Discord Username and Display Name — Collected at interaction time for display purposes in rank cards, leaderboards, welcome messages, and similar rendered content.
  • Discord Avatar URL — Used solely for generating image-based cards. Avatar images are fetched from Discord's CDN and are not stored permanently.
  • Voice Audio Data — If you opt in to the Voice Speech-to-Text (STT) or Voice Cloning features, short audio clips from your microphone are captured. These are sent to third-party services for processing and are not stored on our servers beyond active processing.
  • Message Content — When you interact with the Bot via prefix commands, message content is read to parse your command intent. Message content is not logged or stored beyond the moment of processing, except where explicitly noted.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • XP and Level Data — Awarded automatically based on your activity.
  • Economy Data — Coin balances, gem balances, wallet data, bank account details, and streak information.
  • Game Statistics — Win/loss/draw records, earnings, streaks, and last-played timestamps.
  • Achievement and Quest Progress — Which achievements you have unlocked and your progress.
  • AI Chat History — Per-channel conversation history used to give the AI model context. Stored in our database bounded by a configurable window.
  • Time Capsule Messages — Stored until the scheduled delivery date.
  • Auto-Quote Archives — If enabled in a server, selected messages are stored.
  • Welcome/Leave Configuration — Guild-level settings and templates.
  • Voice Clone Samples — If opted in, a short audio sample is stored locally and submitted to the Murf API until you request deletion.
  • Server Configuration — Guild-level settings are stored and attributed to the guild ID.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Full message history or passive surveillance of channels.
  • Passwords, email addresses, phone numbers, or any account credential.
  • Payment information of any kind.
  • IP addresses or device identifiers.
  • Data from users who have never interacted with the Bot.
